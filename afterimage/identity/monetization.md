# AFTERIMAGE — Monetization Path
## 0 followers to 1M. Revenue from attention, not customers.

---

## THE CORE PRINCIPLE

You do not sell to your audience.
You do not ask anything of your audience.
You do not interrupt the universe with commerce.

Revenue comes from what the universe generates — passively. The content is the asset. The audience is the distribution. The creator never appears.

---

## STAGE 1: BUILDING (0 – 10K followers)
### Revenue: $0
### Focus: Universe establishment, algorithm seeding

Nothing to monetize here. This is pure investment.

**What you're building:**
- The lore infrastructure (posts 1-30)
- The obsessed viewer base (the first 500 people who'll share everything)
- The comment ecosystem (the first theories, the first viral screenshot)

**Metric that matters:** Not followers. **Comments per video.** If you're getting more comments than views (expressed as a ratio), the obsession mechanism is working. Early obsessed viewers make the algorithm take you seriously.

**Cost:** $0 (free tools only)
**Time investment:** 4-6 hours per week (filming, editing, lore tracking)

---

## STAGE 2: PROOF (10K – 50K followers)
### Revenue: $0–$500/month (Creator Fund + platform payments)
### Focus: Viral moment, lore depth, first revenue

**TikTok Creator Rewards Program** (formerly Creator Fund):
- Requires 10K followers + 100K views in last 30 days
- Pays $0.02–$0.04 per 1,000 views (low, but passive)
- At 10K followers with strong engagement: ~$20-100/month
- Apply immediately at 10K

**Instagram Reels Bonus:**
- Instagram periodically offers bonus programs for Reels creators
- At 10K+: apply for whatever program is currently active
- Terms change frequently — watch for updates

**The viral moment:**
- Likely to occur somewhere in Arc 2 (posts 13-28) as the lore gets dense enough for the obsessed viewer to become a evangelist
- One video breaking into broad distribution can move 10K to 50K in days
- You cannot force this — you can only ensure the infrastructure is ready

**At 50K:** The account has enough weight to begin the next stage.

---

## STAGE 3: EXPANSION (50K – 200K followers)
### Revenue: $500–$3,000/month
### Focus: Platform diversification, first premium layer

**Primary revenue streams at this stage:**

**1. TikTok Creator Rewards**
At 50K with strong engagement: $200-500/month from views alone.

**2. YouTube Shorts**
Begin posting the same content to YouTube Shorts at 50K.
YouTube Shorts monetization pays more than TikTok at scale.
No additional production work — same files, different platform.

**3. Brand alignment (selective, universe-consistent)**
At 50K, brands begin approaching cinematic/aesthetic accounts.
Policy: only brands that can become part of the universe without breaking it.
Examples of fits: ambient music platforms, photography apps, fashion brands with the right aesthetic, specific beverage/fragrance with the right visual fit.
Examples of hard no: fast food, tech products, anything that would destroy the anonymous fiction.

Fee at this stage: $500–$2,000 per integration.

**4. Print / physical (optional)**
The AFTERIMAGE universe has strong still-frame potential:
- Prints of the key Anchor frames (the 23rd floor window, the Overpass)
- The cassette as an artifact (physical cassette sold as limited object)
- Giclee prints, limited editions

Platform: Printful (print-on-demand, no inventory, free to set up) integrated with a Shopify Free trial or Etsy.
Price point: $40-150 per print. At 50K followers, even 0.1% conversion = meaningful revenue.

## STAGE 4: SCALE (200K – 1M followers)
### Revenue: $3,000–$20,000+/month
### Focus: Platform revenue + licensing + lore extension

**Platform revenue at 200K+:**
- TikTok Creator Rewards: $1,500–5,000/month (dependent on view rates)
- YouTube Shorts monetization: $500–2,000/month
- Instagram (bonus programs): variable

**Brand deals at 200K+:**
- $3,000–$10,000 per integration (selective)
- By this stage you can dictate the terms — only universe-consistent brands

**Licensing (the long-game play):**
The AFTERIMAGE universe, if it reaches 200K+ followers with an active obsessed fanbase, has value as intellectual property:
- Short film adaptation
- Music video universe (a musician licenses the aesthetic for their visual identity)
- A limited editorial collaboration (fashion magazine, literary journal)
- Streaming platform interest (at 500K+, this becomes realistic — the Webisode/series format)

**The $1M milestone pathway:**
At 1M followers, AFTERIMAGE can generate:
- $5,000–15,000/month passively from platform revenue
- $10,000–30,000/month from brand deals (if selective but consistent)
- IP licensing events: $50,000–$200,000 one-time for rights to adapt
- Merchandise at scale: $2,000–10,000/month (prints, objects, digital downloads)

**Total at 1M:** $20,000–$50,000/month possible. Solo. Faceless. Automated posting cadence.

## THE PATREON / MEMBERSHIP OPTION (Use Carefully)

A membership tier can work for a universe like this IF:
- It's positioned as **extended universe access**, not "support the creator"
- The Patreon contains lore that doesn't appear on TikTok (deeper handwriting notes, the internal canon, extended cuts)
- The creator still never appears
- The membership is called something universe-consistent ("The Archive" or "The 23rd Floor" tier)

**When to launch:** At 50K followers minimum. Before that, it signals desperation.
**Price:** $5/month (entry) + $15/month (deeper archive access)
**Conversion rate at 50K:** Expect 0.5-2% = 250–1,000 members = $1,250–$15,000/month

This is the highest-leverage revenue path because it converts the obsessed viewer — the most valuable segment — into a recurring revenue stream.

---

## WHAT YOU DO NOT DO

- No merchandise with the creator's name/identity
- No Discord server with the creator as a presence (the audience can build one — you don't operate it)
- No podcast or extended-format content where the creator speaks as themselves
- No brand deals that require the creator to appear on camera
- No livestreams
- No "collab" videos with other creators that break the fictional universe

The universe is the brand. Every monetization decision is judged against one question: **does this break the universe?**

If yes: decline, regardless of the money.
If no: proceed.

The audience pays attention because the universe is consistent and protected. The moment it breaks, they leave. The universe is the only asset that matters.
