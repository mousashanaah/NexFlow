# AFTERIMAGE — Account Identity & Setup

---

## ACCOUNT NAMES

**Primary (TikTok):** @afterimage.city or @afterimage__ or @.afterimage
**Instagram Reels mirror:** Same handle

**If taken:** @afterimage.urban / @afterimage.unknown / @afterimage_city

The word "afterimage" is the phenomenon where you see the ghost of an image after the source is gone. It is the exact psychological metaphor for the content. Do not compromise on this name.

**Display name:** afterimage (lowercase, always)

---

## BIO

**TikTok:**
```
fragments of an unnamed city.
```
That's it. Nothing else. No links until monetization begins. No email. No explanation.

**Instagram:**
```
fragments of an unnamed city.
```
Identical. The account speaks with one voice.

**Why this works:**
The bio performs the same function as the content — it withholds. "Fragments" implies incompleteness. "Unnamed city" implies the city matters more than who made this. The period creates finality. The audience arrives and the bio is the first mystery.

## PROFILE IMAGE

A single frame from the universe. Suggestions:
- The 23rd floor window (from outside, night, warm light)
- The cassette tape, close-up, on a dark surface
- The city at night from the Overpass, protagonist absent

**Rules:**
- No face, no logo, no text
- Consistent with the AFTERIMAGE color grade (the same amber/teal palette)
- Never change it for at least 6 months. Stability is identity at this scale.

## THE ACCOUNT VOICE

The account can comment (rarely) or respond (almost never). When it does:

**Never:**
- First person ("I" or "we")
- Creator-speak ("this video means...")
- Direct lore answers
- Enthusiasm ("omg love this comment!!")
- Acknowledgment of being a creator ("made this at 2am lol")

**If ever:**
- One fragment, lowercase, no punctuation except periods
- Could be the universe speaking, not the creator
- Examples:
  - "some things stay"
  - "the light was on that night"
  - "she came back"
  - [single emoji: the moon, or a window, or static — ◽ or 🌙 or 📼]

The account is the universe. The creator does not exist.

## ANONYMITY ARCHITECTURE

No real name. No face. No location revealed.

**Email for account creation:** New Proton Mail — afterimage@protonmail.com or similar
**Phone for verification:** Use a VoIP number (TextNow free tier) — never your real number
**Payment (for monetization, when it comes):** Stripe with a business entity, or PayPal
**Legal:** When revenue grows past $1K/month, consider an LLC for liability insulation

**The test:** could someone watching every video and reading every comment determine who made this? The answer must always be no.

---

## AESTHETIC CONSISTENCY ACROSS PLATFORMS

TikTok and Instagram must feel identical:
- Same video (same export, same aspect ratio 9:16)
- Same caption (word for word)
- Same hashtags (3 max, always the same 3: #afterimage #nightcity #shortfilm)
- Same comment behavior (minimal)

**Cross-platform posting tool:** CapCut has direct export to both. Or use Buffer (free tier) for scheduling.

---

## ACCOUNT AGE AND LAUNCH TIMING

Do not post immediately after account creation. Let the account sit for 3-5 days with only a profile image — this seeds the algorithm's audience model before the first video.

**First post:** Tuesday, 7pm–9pm local time (consistently the strongest window for atmospheric content)
**Second post:** Friday, same window
**Establish the pattern before the 4th post** — TikTok rewards regularity in the first 30 days.
