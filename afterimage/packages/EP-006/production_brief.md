# EP-006 — Production Brief
**Anchor:** The Car
**Feeling:** a decision that became permanent without feeling like one
**Type:** Pure Moment
**Duration:** 18 seconds
**Cassette:** absent
**Other One:** FIRST IMPLIED PRESENCE (passenger seat — empty, but not neutral)

---

## CORE MOMENT
She is in the car. Moving. The passenger seat is empty, but there's a jacket on it. The car moves through the city. She doesn't look at the jacket. She doesn't look anywhere except forward.

## WHAT IS WITHHELD
Where she's going. Whose jacket. Whether she's leaving or arriving. Whether she is aware of the jacket.

## EMOTIONAL ARC
0:00 — Moving. Direction unknown.
0:18 — Still moving. The jacket was always there. You just noticed it. She already knew.

---

## SHOT LIST

**Part 1 — Establishing Texture (0:00–0:02)**
The dashboard. Close-up. The amber light of the instruments. The texture of the steering wheel at the edge of frame. The windshield with city lights smearing past.

**Part 2 — Figure in Negative Space (0:02–0:04)**
Pull back: she is driving, from behind/side — we see her from the back seat angle. Her hands on the wheel. The city streaming past outside. The passenger seat is visible in this frame. A jacket on it. Nothing else.

**Part 3 — Movement Initiation (0:04–0:05)**
The city outside changes — a longer stretch of darkness, then: neon. The neon light sweeps across the interior. For 1 second, the jacket is lit amber-orange. She doesn't react.

**Part 4 — Tempo-Visual Coupling (0:05–0:07)**
A musical tone arrives as the neon sweeps across. The light passes. The interior returns to dark. The jacket is there.

**Part 5 — Compression Moment (0:07–0:08)**
Close-up: the jacket on the passenger seat. Amber light from the dashboard just catching it. No one in the seat. The seatbelt unfastened. The jacket is just there.

**Part 6 — Emotional Peak Image (0:08–0:09)**
Her hands on the wheel, the passenger seat jacket in the middle distance, the city streaming outside the windows behind both. She and the jacket and the moving city. The screenshot frame.

**Part 7 — Release (0:09–0:10)**
Her right hand lifts briefly from the wheel — a gesture that might be reaching toward the jacket — and returns. The city continues. Cut.

**Part 8 — Audio Decay (0:10–0:18)**
City driving sound continues (road noise, distant city) over black for 8 seconds. The car is still moving in the audio. Then: it stops. Silence.

**Grade:** AFTERIMAGE_MASTER + Car script (neutral dark interior, amber dashboard, cold city blue through windows — the interior/exterior split)
