## CORE MOMENT
The Overpass again. Same spot. Same railing. But this time: the cassette is in her hand, resting on the railing. She came back here with it.

## WHAT IS WITHHELD
Why she brought it here. Whether she played it here. Whether she comes here because of it or in spite of it.

## EMOTIONAL ARC
0:00 — The overpass. You've seen this before.
0:15 — But she has the cassette now. She didn't last time. Something changed. Or something didn't.

## SHOT LIST

**Part 1 — Establishing Texture (0:00–0:02)**
The railing — same texture as EP-001's opening shot. Cold blue-gray steel. But this time: the cassette is visible on the railing. It's there before we see her.

**Part 2 — Figure in Negative Space (0:02–0:04)**
Same framing as EP-001 Part 2. She is at the railing, from behind. The city behind her. But we can now see: her right hand is around the cassette on the railing.

**Part 3 — Movement Initiation (0:04–0:05)**
She doesn't grip the railing this time. She picks up the cassette. One hand, lifts it.

**Part 4 — Tempo-Visual Coupling (0:05–0:07)**
Cut to: looking down at the city — same shot as EP-001 Part 4. The music arrives. The city continues. But now: the cassette is reflected in the wet railing below — a small dark rectangle.

**Part 5 — Compression Moment (0:07–0:08)**
Her hands with the cassette, same framing as EP-004. The railing is visible at the bottom of frame. The city is outside the frame. She is between both.

**Part 6 — Emotional Peak Image (0:08–0:09)**
Her silhouette from behind — same as EP-001 Part 6. But her hands are different: one holds the cassette at her chest, not the railing. The city behind. The screenshot frame.

**Part 7 — Release (0:09–0:10)**
She sets the cassette back on the railing. Both hands release it. It stays. Cut.

**Part 8 — Audio Decay (0:10–0:15)**
Black. City ambient. Wind against the overpass structure. 5 seconds. The cassette is up there alone in the audio darkness.
