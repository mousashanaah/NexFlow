# EP-001 — Production Brief
**Anchor:** Overpass
**Feeling:** the weight of a place you can't return to
**Type:** Pure Moment
**Duration:** 15 seconds
**Cassette:** absent
**Other One:** none

---

## CORE MOMENT
She stands at the railing of the Overpass, looking at the city below. She doesn't move. The city moves without her. She leaves before we see why she came.

## WHAT IS WITHHELD
Why she came here. What she was looking for below.

## EMOTIONAL ARC
0:00 — The city. Cold. You are in it.
0:15 — She was here. She's leaving. You don't know what she found.

---

## SHOT LIST

**Part 1 — Establishing Texture (0:00–0:02)**
The wet metal surface of the railing. Extreme close-up. Rain residue pooled in the railing's channel. Cold blue-gray. Nothing else visible. Camera: static.

**Part 2 — Figure in Negative Space (0:02–0:04)**
Pull back to reveal: she is already at the railing, her hands resting on it, back to camera. The city is enormous behind her. She is small. She is already there — we were looking at something else.

**Part 3 — Movement Initiation (0:04–0:05)**
Her hands tighten on the railing. One small grip. That's all. She's holding on.

**Part 4 — Tempo-Visual Coupling (0:05–0:07)**
Cut to: looking down at the city from her height. The lights below. A musical tone arrives on this cut — something quiet, something sustained. The city continues.

**Part 5 — Compression Moment (0:07–0:08)**
Back to: her hands on the railing, extreme close-up. The knuckle detail. The cold metal under warm skin. One second only.

**Part 6 — Emotional Peak Image (0:08–0:09)**
Her silhouette from behind, city behind her, both railing hands visible, hair catching the cold city glow from below. This is the screenshot frame.

**Part 7 — Release (0:09–0:10)**
She releases the railing. One hand lifts. Cut — before we see what she does with it.

**Part 8 — Audio Decay (0:10–0:15)**
Black frame. The ambient city audio continues: distant traffic, wind, electrical hum. Fades over 5 seconds. Silence.
