# EP-002 — Production Brief
**Anchor:** Convenience Store
**Feeling:** being alone in a place designed for people who are not alone
**Type:** Pure Moment
**Duration:** 12 seconds
**Cassette:** absent
**Other One:** none

---

## CORE MOMENT
She stands at the glass door of the convenience store at night. The fluorescent interior is visible behind the glass. She sees her own reflection. She goes in. We don't follow.

## WHAT IS WITHHELD
What she needed. What she found. Whether she stayed.

## EMOTIONAL ARC
0:00 — The store exists. Ordinary, lit, indifferent.
0:12 — She entered it. The ordinary received her. That's all.

---

## SHOT LIST

**Part 1 — Establishing Texture (0:00–0:02)**
The wet pavement outside the store. The fluorescent green-white light spilling across it. The reflection of the sign in the puddle. Static, looking down.

**Part 2 — Figure in Negative Space (0:02–0:04)**
Pull back: she is outside the glass door, looking in. Back to camera. The store interior reflects over her reflection. She is double — outside and reflected inside simultaneously.

**Part 3 — Movement Initiation (0:04–0:05)**
Her hand comes up and touches the glass door. Not pushing it open. Just touching it.

**Part 4 — Tempo-Visual Coupling (0:05–0:07)**
Cut to: the interior seen through the glass from outside. The empty aisles. The overhead fluorescent. A music arrival — something unresolved. The store continues being a store.

**Part 5 — Compression Moment (0:07–0:08)**
Her reflection in the glass. Close. The reflection is clearer than she is. Backward world in the glass.

**Part 6 — Emotional Peak Image (0:08–0:09)**
Her silhouette against the green-white glow of the store. Full body, from behind, door handle visible beside her hand, the lit interior behind her. The screenshot frame.

**Part 7 — Release (0:09–0:10)**
She pushes the door. It opens. She enters. Cut — she is half through the door, the frame cuts.

**Part 8 — Audio Decay (0:10–0:12)**
Black. The sound of the door's entry chime continues for 1 second after black. Then city ambient. Then silence.

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES
**Mode:** Filmed strongly preferred — real convenience stores have irreplaceable fluorescent character
**Music direction:** Ambient, slightly irregular pulse — like a fluorescent light that almost hums. Arrives quietly at 0:05. Doesn't resolve.
**Grade:** AFTERIMAGE_MASTER + Convenience Store script (green-white fluorescent, shadow floor becomes green-black #0f1a0f)
