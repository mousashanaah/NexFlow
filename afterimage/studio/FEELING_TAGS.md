# AFTERIMAGE — Feeling Tag Library
## The emotional input that drives every episode. Select one per run.

---

## HOW TO USE

The feeling tag is the single most important input to the pipeline.
It is the difference between generic atmospheric content and content that stops someone mid-scroll.

Rule: Never repeat a feeling tag within 8 episodes.
Rule: One feeling tag per episode. Not a combination.
Rule: If none of these fit what you want to express — write a new one in the same syntax.

The syntax is: **a specific emotional experience described in plain language, from the inside.**
Not "sadness." Not "longing." The exact texture of one particular kind of sadness or longing.

---

## THE LIBRARY (36 Tags — ~4 months of content)

### On Absence
```
"the weight of a place you can't return to"
"the specific silence of an empty space that used to have someone in it"
"knowing something is over before the last time happens"
"an ending that arrived without an announcement"
"the version of yourself that existed in one specific place, that you can't access anymore"
```

### On Time
```
"recognizing the last time only after it's passed"
"the ordinary moment you didn't know to remember"
"a year that felt like a week while it was happening"
"the strange continuity of places that don't know what happened in them"
"moving forward while part of you is still in the same room"
```

### On Cities and Night
```
"3am in a city that doesn't know you"
"the city as witness to things it can't help you with"
"being alone in a place designed for people who are not alone"
"the hour when the city stops pretending to be busy"
"a familiar street that became a different street"
```

### On Connection
```
"wanting to call someone and not doing it"
"what you carry instead of saying"
"a message you never sent becoming a thing that defines you"
"the distance between what you felt and what you said"
"missing someone who is not gone, exactly"
```

### On Decisions
```
"a decision that became permanent without feeling like one"
"something ended without a ceremony"
"the moment you knew, before you admitted knowing"
"the version of events where you stayed"
"a choice that felt small when you made it"
```

### On Searching
```
"looking for evidence of someone in the places they used to be"
"returning to see if it still feels the same"
"wanting one more version of something that can't be revised"
"the habit of reaching for something that isn't there"
"being in the right place at the wrong time of your life"
```

### On Feeling
```
"a feeling that has no arrival time and no departure"
"something you can't locate but can't stop carrying"
"the hour when you stop pretending it's fine"
"knowing something is wrong in a way that has no name"
"being more honest at 2am than you've been in months"
```

---

## ROTATION SYSTEM

Maintain a simple list in the Notion episode log:
- Column: `feeling_tag`
- Before each run, check the last 8 entries
- Select a tag from a different category than the last 2 entries

This ensures emotional variety without drift — the universe stays in its register, but never repeats the same texture twice in a row.

---

## WRITING NEW TAGS

When the library needs expansion, the format is:
- Second-person or neutral ("you" or "the")
- Present tense or gerund
- Specific, not categorical
- Max 12 words
- Must be recognizable as a universal experience without being named

Bad: "sadness about the past"
Good: "the version of yourself that existed in one specific place, that you can't access anymore"

Bad: "loneliness at night"  
Good: "being alone in a place designed for people who are not alone"
