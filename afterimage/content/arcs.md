# AFTERIMAGE — First 50 Posts Mapped

---

## ARC STRUCTURE

The 50 posts are organized into 4 arcs. Each arc has a different emotional register and lore function. The arcs are invisible to casual viewers. They are legible only to those who've watched everything in sequence — which is the reward.

---

## ARC 1: THE WORLD (Posts 1-12)
### Register: Atmospheric, quiet, no urgency
### Function: Establish the universe before establishing the mystery

The audience learns: the city, the Anchors, the protagonist's texture, the palette.
No answers. No explicit questions yet. Just: this world exists and it feels like something.

Lore seeds planted but not activated: Cassette (x2), 23rd Floor (x2), Signal (x2), Handwriting (x1), The Other One (x1, implied only).

**Post breakdown:**
- Posts 1-2: Pure Moment (Anchors only — Overpass, Convenience Store)
- Post 3: Anchor Visit (Stairwell — Signal planted)
- Post 4: Pure Moment (Cassette first appearance)
- Post 5: Fragment (23rd Floor window)
- Post 6: Pure Moment (The Car — Other One implied)
- Post 7: Echo (Post #1 location, cassette now visible)
- Post 8: Pure Moment (The Note / Handwriting)
- Post 9: Anchor Visit (Apartment Window)
- Post 10: Interruption (23rd Floor light out — first shift)
- Post 11: Pure Moment (Back to the Overpass — she stays longer this time)
- Post 12: Fragment (The Signal appears explicitly for 2 seconds — audience starts noticing)

**Arc 1 ends:** The world feels inhabited. The audience feels something is being withheld but hasn't named it yet.

### Register: Searching energy. She is moving toward something.
### Function: Introduce the central question without naming it

The audience learns: something happened. She is looking for something or someone. The Anchors feel different to her now. The handwriting appears more frequently. The Other One becomes more defined through absence.

The audience begins theorizing around Post 16-18 as patterns become undeniable.

**Post breakdown:**

- Post 16: Echo (The Stairwell from Post 3 — but she's in it now. The Signal appears while she's there.)

- Post 18: Pure Moment (The Car again — this time the driver's seat has something in it. A jacket.)
- Post 19: Anchor Visit (The 23rd Floor exterior — full video. She stands outside the building. Looks up. Doesn't enter.)
- Post 20: Pure Moment (The Cassette — she tries to play it. We don't hear what's on it. She stops it.)
- Post 21: Fragment (2:47am on a clock — first explicit timestamp appearance)

- Post 23: Pure Moment (The Apartment Window — but there's a reflection of someone behind her. She doesn't turn.)
- Posts 24-25: Pure Moments (city establishing — she moves through it differently now)
- Post 26: Interruption (She reaches the 23rd floor elevator button. Stops. Doesn't press 23.)
- Post 27: Fragment (The coat from Post 6's car — on a different chair. Different location.)

**Arc 2 ends:** The audience knows there's a mystery and knows exactly which pieces belong to it. The theories begin. The lore archaeology is active.

## ARC 4: THE RETURN (Posts 43-50)
### Register: Quiet after the peak. Different kind of weight.
### Function: Establish that the universe continues — resolution is not the point

After the interruption of Post 42, Arc 4 steps back. Not resolution. A different phase.

New information: whether she went through the door is never shown. The next videos are set in the same world, but something about her position in it has shifted. The audience decides what that means.

**Post breakdown:**
- Post 43: Pure Moment (The Overpass, night. She's there. Nothing else. The cassette is not in her hands.)
- Post 44: Anchor Visit (The Convenience Store — late, almost closing. She's inside for the first time.)
- Post 45: Fragment (The door to the 23rd floor — from the outside. No hand. Just the door.)
- Post 46: Echo (Post 1 — the very first Overpass shot. Identical framing. But she's walking away now.)
- Post 47: Pure Moment (The city in a new light — not dawn exactly, but the quality of air before dawn.)
- Post 48: Fragment (The Handwriting one more time: "i know you know")
- Post 49: Anchor Visit (All 6 Anchors in sequence — very fast cuts. Each one, 2 seconds. She's not in any of them. They exist without her.)
- Post 50: Pure Moment (She's in the car. Moving. The city is behind her. This could be leaving. Or arriving. Cut.)

**Arc 4 ends:** Open. The universe continues. The audience has everything and nothing. The central question has not been answered. The lore is rich enough to sustain another 50 posts.
