## WHAT AFTERIMAGE IS

A short-form content universe operating on TikTok and Instagram Reels.
No face. No voice. No creator identity.

The content depicts fragments of an unnamed woman's life in an unnamed city —
always at night, always slightly wrong, always leaving something out.

The audience does not watch AFTERIMAGE. They investigate it.

---

## THE CORE MECHANISM (From Research)

Every piece of content that generates obsession operates on the same underlying principle:
**the brain cannot stop processing incomplete patterns.**

The Kershaw moment works because it withholds resolution at the exact moment tension peaks.
Dreamcore works because spaces almost-match memory but don't — the brain keeps checking.
Entergalactic works because the city has feelings the characters never name.

AFTERIMAGE uses all three simultaneously:
- **Temporal incompleteness** (Dreamcore): spaces and times that feel dislocated
- **Emotional incompleteness** (Kershaw): peaks that cut before the release
- **World incompleteness** (Entergalactic): a city larger than any video shows

The audience fills the gaps. Filling the gaps creates investment. Investment creates comments, theories, rewatches.

---

## THE ONE RULE

**Every video must end before it's over.**

Not cliffhangers. Not "part 2." Not teasing.
The ending must feel like the beginning of something you didn't know was coming.
