## DEFINITIONS

| Metric | Counts | Does NOT Count |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Outreach Sent | Reddit posts + DMs sent + Twitter threads | Upvotes, reposts |
| Responses | Any reply, DM, comment response | Upvotes, likes, passive engagement |
| Conversations | 3+ message exchange where they answer a workflow question | "Cool idea" replies |
| Positive | Describes real pain AND expresses interest in the digest | Interest without pain |
| WTP Signal | Asks about pricing unprompted, says "I'd pay for this," completes intake form | Enthusiasm, "sounds interesting" |
| Paying | Credit card on file, Stripe shows a charge | Everything else |

## DECISION GATES

### Day 7 Checkpoint
- Conversations ≥ 3 → CONTINUE
- Conversations < 3 → Diagnose: distribution problem or product problem?

### Day 14 Checkpoint
- Positive responses ≥ 3 OR WTP signals ≥ 2 → Build delivery system, continue outreach
- Neither met → Pivot channel or digest format before continuing

### Day 30 Decision

| Outcome | Decision |
|---------|----------|
| 1+ paying customer | **GO** — build the full pipeline |
| 3+ WTP signals, 0 paying | Test $49/month, 2-week extension |
| 5+ conversations, clear pain, no WTP | Pricing or trust issue — diagnose before killing |
| <3 conversations, "SAM.gov is fine" | **KILL** — move to next intelligence niche |
| <3 conversations, nobody engaged | Distribution problem, not product — fix channel |
